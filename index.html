Problem & Solution

The Widespread Problem

In Uzbekistan, the rapidly growing banking sector offers countless services—from mortgages and auto loans to savings accounts, investment products, and credit cards. However, most people lack the financial literacy to navigate this complex landscape effectively. This creates a critical gap where citizens:

  • Rely on unreliable advice from friends, family, or salespeople with conflicting interests
  • Fall victim to deceptive practices through misleading percentage calculations and hidden fees
  • Make poor financial decisions that lead to overwhelming debt or missed opportunities
  • Waste countless hours researching and comparing options across multiple banks
  • Develop distrust toward financial institutions due to bad experiences

This problem affects everyone—from students getting their first card to families buying their dream home to retirees planning their savings.

Our Complete Solution

Besty is Uzbekistan's first AI-powered financial companion that provides unbiased, data-driven advice for every banking service. We will build an intelligent assistant that:

  • Analyzes your complete financial picture through natural conversation
  • Scans and compares all available options across every bank in Uzbekistan
  • Explains complex terms and calculations in simple, clear language
  • Provides personalized recommendations based solely on your best interests
  • Answers follow-up questions and guides you through entire processes
  • Protects you from bad decisions by warning when services don't fit your situation

Whether you're applying for a mortgage, choosing a savings account, selecting a credit card, or exploring investment options—Besty ensures you make informed, confident decisions.
